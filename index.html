How dominant language is calculated

The Compass calculates dominant language from your data — no manual entry needed. If Spanish percentile exceeds English by more than 10 points, the student is Spanish Dominant. If English exceeds Spanish by more than 10 points, English Dominant. Within 10 points of each other = Balanced/Emerging.
